How It Works

By uploading a picture of a stray animal, you help us track and manage the safety of animals on the streets. Here's how it works:

  1. Enter the Location: Provide the location where the stray animal was spotted. This helps in mapping the animals' positions for better monitoring.
  2. Select a Picture: Choose a clear picture of the animal. This image will be stored in our database for further analysis and tracking.
  3. Upload: Once you submit the form, the picture will be uploaded, and the location information will be saved for future reference and action.

Your contribution plays a vital role in helping prevent road accidents and ensuring the safety of stray animals. Thank you for your support!
